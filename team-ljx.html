About Me

Jiaxu Leng

Jiaxu Leng, Associate Professor with the School of Computer Science and Technology, Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications （CQUPT）, Chongqing, China. Dr. Leng is the director of the Trusted Visual Intelligence Group (TVI Group), which is affiliated with the Smart Mind and Super Vision Group. He received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Application Technology from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China, in 2020. His current research interests include Computer Vision, Face Super-Resolution, Person Re-Identification, Video Anomaly Detection, Autonomous Driving, and Embodied Intelligence. In the past three years, he has led one General Program and one Young Scientists Fund project from the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), one General Program from the China Postdoctoral Science Foundation, and four provincial or ministerial-level general/key projects. Currently, he has published over 40 papers in NeurIPS, ACM CSUR, ACM MM, IEEE TIFS, IEEE TMM, IEEETITS, IEEE TCSVT, IEEE TGRS, etc. He has served as the Local Chair of VALSE 2024 and has served as the reviewer for prestigious journals and conferences, including CVPR, ECCV, IEEE TIP, and IEEE TMM.
