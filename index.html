Trustworthy Visual Intelligence Group

The Trustworthy Visual Intelligence (TVI) group is a core part of the Image Cognition Group at Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications. Founded in 2020, we are a dynamic research team led by Associate Professor Jiaxu Leng .

GARDEN: Smart Sanitation Model

Garden adopts a collaborative architecture of 'small perception model + large multimodal model'. It enables precise garbage recognition, road cleanliness evaluation, and intelligent scheduling across multiple scenarios in complex terrains.
